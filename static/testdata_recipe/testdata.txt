Name: Fried Rice

Ingredients:
- Cooked rice
- Vegetable oil
- Eggs
- Mixed vegetables
- Garlic
- Green onions
- Soy sauce
- Oyster sauce



Instructions:
1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
2. Add the beaten eggs to the skillet and scramble them until cooked through. Remove and set aside.
3. Add more vegetable oil to the skillet if needed.
4. Stir in minced garlic and cook until fragrant.
5. Add mixed vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry until tender.
6. Add the cooked rice to the skillet, breaking up any clumps, and stir-fry.
7. Pour soy sauce and oyster sauce (if using) over the rice and vegetables. Stir well to combine.
8. Add the scrambled eggs back to the skillet and stir-fry to heat through.
9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
10. Garnish with sliced green onions before serving.
11. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious homemade fried rice!

Chicken Soup
Ingredients:
- chicken
- Mixed vegetables
- rice
- Broth
- herbs

Instructions:
1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
2. Sauté the vegetables until they are softened.
3. Add the chicken meat to the pot and cook until heated through.
4. Pour in enough broth to cover the ingredients in the pot.
5. Bring the soup to a simmer and let it cook for about 15-20 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
6. Add cooked rice or pasta to the soup.
7. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs according to taste.
8. Let the soup simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes.
9. Serve hot and enjoy your comforting chicken soup!



Roasted Vegetable Frittata
Ingredients:
- Roasted vegetables
- Cooked meat
- Eggs
- Milk
- Grated cheese
- Olive oil

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Grease a cast-iron skillet or oven-safe pan with olive oil.
3. Spread the roasted vegetables and cooked meat (diced evenly in the skillet.
4. In a mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, milk or cream, grated cheese, salt, and pepper.
5. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables and meat in the skillet.
6. Gently stir to ensure the ingredients are evenly distributed.
7. Place the skillet in the preheated oven and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the frittata is set and golden brown on top.
8. Once cooked, remove the frittata from the oven and let it cool slightly.
9. Slice the frittata into wedges and serve warm or at room temperature.
10. Enjoy your delicious roast vegetable frittata as a hearty meal or snack!



Chicken Stir Fry
Ingredients:
- Cooked chicken
- Mixed vegetables
- Cooked rice
- Soy sauce
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Olive oil
- Onions

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil or sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger to the skillet, sauté until fragrant.
3. Toss in the mixed vegetables and stir-fry until they are tender yet crisp.
4. Add the cooked chicken (shredded or sliced) to the skillet and stir-fry until heated through.
5. Add cooked rice to the skillet and stir well to combine.
6. Pour soy sauce over the mixture and continue to stir-fry for a few more minutes.
7. Season with salt and pepper according to taste.
8. Garnish with chopped green onions if desired.
9. Serve hot and enjoy your flavorful chicken stir-fry!



Ham and Cheese Casserole
Ingredients:
- Cooked ham
- Bread slices
- Eggs
- Milk
- Cheddar cheese

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Grease a baking dish with butter or cooking spray.
3. Spread bread cubes and diced ham evenly in the baking dish.
4. In a mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
5. Pour the egg mixture over the bread cubes and ham.
6. Sprinkle grated cheddar cheese over the top.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes or until set and golden brown.
8. Let it cool slightly before slicing and serving.
9. Enjoy your hearty ham and cheese breakfast casserole!



Shepherd's Pie
Ingredients:
- Cooked beef
- Mixed vegetables
- Mashed potatoes
- cheddar cheese
- Worcestershire sauce

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Spread cooked ground beef or lamb evenly in a baking dish.
3. Layer mixed vegetables over the meat.
4. Drizzle Worcestershire sauce over the vegetables.
5. Spread mashed potatoes over the top, covering the filling completely.
6. Sprinkle grated cheddar cheese over the mashed potatoes.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
8. Let it cool slightly before serving.
9. Enjoy your comforting shepherd's pie!



Chicken Tacos

Vegetarian Pasta Primavera
Ingredients:
- Mixed Vegetables
- Pasta
- Olive oil
- Garlic
- Parmesan cheese
- basil leaves

Instructions:
1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
3. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
4. Add assorted vegetables to the skillet and sauté until tender.
5. Toss cooked pasta with the vegetables in the skillet.
6. Season with Italian seasoning and toss to combine.
7. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh basil leaves if desired.
8. Enjoy your delightful vegetarian pasta primavera!


Apple Crisp
Ingredients:
- Apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
- Brown sugar
- All-purpose flour
- Rolled oats
- Ground cinnamon
- Ground nutmeg
- Butter

Instructions:
1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a large bowl, combine sliced apples with brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
3. Spread the apple mixture evenly in a baking dish.
4. In another bowl, mix together flour, rolled oats, and a bit more cinnamon.
5. Cut in cold cubed butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
6. Sprinkle the crumb mixture evenly over the apples.
7. Bake in the preheated oven for about 30-35 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apples are tender.
8. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream.
9. Enjoy your comforting apple crisp dessert!
